Welcome to

St.Thomas Cathedral Basilica

The Shrine serves as a sacred place of worship, pilgrimage, evangelization, and reconciliation, fulfilling its mission with great dedication. We trust that you will find the information on this site valuable, especially if you are planning a pilgrimage to our Shrine. You can conveniently access our schedules and submit prayer requests online.

As the heart of our parish, the Shrine serves as the spiritual home to nearly 1,600 families. It is a vibrant community of faith, welcoming over 200,000 visitors each year. Open year-round, the Shrine receives close to one million pilgrims and tourists annually, drawing people from across the country and around the globe. We offer three daily Masses and more than five hours of confessions each day, along with a Catholic bookstore to serve the needs of our visitors.

Throughout the year, the Shrine hosts special Masses, devotions, and concerts, with additional celebrations on Holy Days and holidays. Please check our schedule for further details!

Prayer in Honour Of St.Thomas

O Lord God! We thank and praise you for sending St.Thomas the Apostle, in your divine providence to India, for proclaiming the good news that Jesus is our Lord and Saviour. He not only confessed Jesus as his Lord and Saviour, he also sealed his faith with his blood, and became the first martyr of the Indian Church. We glorify you Lord, for inspiring us through the courageous witness of St.Thomas.
