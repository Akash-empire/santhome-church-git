Rev. Fr. Vincent Chinnadurai

Rector & Parish Priest

Date of Birth : 16-September-1953
Date of Ordination : 27-April-1986

A devoted follower of the Blessed Virgin Mary, he humbly serves the parish, ensuring that all parishioners are cared for and spiritually nourished. With a heart open to every pilgrim, he listens attentively and offers compassionate counsel, reflecting Christ’s love through his words and actions.

His deep devotion to St. Mother Teresa is evident in his spiritual and pastoral approach. He embodies her spirit of service and humility, endearing himself to all through his commitment to the Gospel. His close association with St. Mother Teresa has profoundly shaped his ministry, as he continues to live out her legacy of love and compassion for all.

Rev. Fr. Arockia Wilson

The Administrator

Date of Birth : 09-March-1990
Date of Ordination : 12-December-2020

A dedicated and joyful priest, he tirelessly works to foster the spiritual growth and well-being of all those entrusted to his care. His ministry is marked by meticulous attention to detail and a spirit of innovation, continually seeking new and meaningful ways to deepen the faith of the community.

Alongside his pastoral duties, he excels in administrative leadership, ensuring the efficient management of parish operations and resources.

His ability to balance spiritual guidance with administrative responsibilities enables the parish to thrive both as a place of worship and as a well-functioning community. With a pastoral heart and unwavering commitment, he inspires others to embrace their spiritual journey with renewed fervour and devotion.

Rev. Fr. Michael Andrew

Asst Parish Priest

Date of Birth : 9-December-1994
Date of Ordination : 8-June-2025

A great friend of the Youth and he is very friendly with the Parishners.

He is responsible for the effective management of liturgical services, ensuring that each celebration is carried out with reverence and precision. He also plays a key role in guiding the youth ministry, overseeing altar servers, and nurturing various spiritual groups within the parish, fostering their growth and active participation in the life of the Church.

A humble and dedicated servant of Christ, he is known for his hard work, attention to detail, and unwavering commitment to his ministry. His pastoral care, combined with his leadership in the liturgical and spiritual life of the parish, greatly enriches the community's faith journey.

Rev. Fr. Anthony Swamy

Spiritual Counsellor

Date of Birth : 01-January-1940
Date of Ordination : 26-October-1974

An experienced Counsellor and inspite of his age, which he never bothers, he is always ready to listen to Confessions or for Counselling.

A senior priest who has faithfully completed 50 years of Sacerdotal Ministry, he continues to serve with great dedication as a Spiritual Counselor. Despite his many years of service, he remains humble and approachable, embodying the simplicity and grace of a true servant of God. His active involvement in the parish reflects his deep commitment to addressing the diverse spiritual needs of the community.
